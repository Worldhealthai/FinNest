Privacy Policy for FinNest

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Overview

FinNest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and full name
  • Financial Data: ISA contribution amounts, provider names, and dates you choose to track
  • All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model and operating system version
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the app

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the FinNest service
  • Track your ISA contributions and allowances
  • Improve and optimize our app
  • Respond to your requests and support needs

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your financial data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, we do not sync your data to cloud servers
  • Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your ISA tracking data remains private and is only accessible on your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of data collection (by not using the app)

Children's Privacy

FinNest is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@finnest.app

Data Retention

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Third-Party Services

FinNest currently does not use third-party analytics or advertising services. If we add these services in the future, we will update this privacy policy accordingly.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using your information depends on the data and the context:

  • You have given us permission to do so
  • Processing is necessary to provide the service you requested
  • We have a legitimate interest in protecting our services

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
