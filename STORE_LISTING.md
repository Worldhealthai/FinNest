## Full Description (4000 characters max)

FinNest is your smart ISA allowance tracker that helps you manage all your Individual Savings Accounts in one beautiful app.

**TRACK ALL YOUR ISAs**
Whether you have Cash ISAs, Stocks & Shares ISAs, Lifetime ISAs, or Innovative Finance ISAs - FinNest keeps everything organized in one place.

**NEVER EXCEED YOUR ALLOWANCE**
With real-time tracking of your £20,000 annual ISA allowance, you'll always know exactly how much you can still invest tax-free this year.

**KEY FEATURES:**
✓ Track multiple ISA types and providers
✓ Monitor your annual £20,000 allowance
✓ See contributions across all your accounts
✓ Tax year countdown and reminders
✓ Lifetime ISA 25% government bonus calculator
✓ Beautiful, intuitive dark mode interface
✓ Flexible ISA withdrawal tracking
✓ Historical contribution analytics
✓ Secure local data storage

**PERFECT FOR:**
• UK savers maximizing their ISA allowances
• People with multiple ISA accounts
• First-time ISA investors
• Anyone planning their tax-free savings strategy
• Financial planners and advisors

**SMART REMINDERS:**
FinNest alerts you when:
- You're approaching your annual limit
- The tax year is ending soon
- You've exceeded your allowance

**PRIVACY FIRST:**
All your financial data stays on your device. No cloud storage, no data sharing, complete privacy.

**GUEST MODE:**
Try the app without signing up! Experience all features in guest mode before creating an account.

**WHY FINNEST?**
Managing multiple ISAs across different providers can be confusing. It's easy to lose track of contributions and accidentally exceed your allowance. FinNest solves this by giving you a single, clear view of all your ISA investments.

One app. Every ISA. See your limits. Grow your future!

Perfect for UK savers who want to make the most of their ISA allowances and build their financial future.
